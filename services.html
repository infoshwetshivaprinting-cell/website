Digital vs. Offset

Helping you choose the right technology for your budget.

Choose Digital if:

  • • You need less than 500 units
  • • You need it within 24-48 hours
  • • Every piece needs unique data (Personalization)
  • • You want to test a design before a bulk order

Choose Offset if:

  • • You need 1,000+ units
  • • You want the lowest "per-unit" cost
  • • You require Pantone/Metallic color accuracy
  • • You are printing on heavy textured cardstock
